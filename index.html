PRISM: Performer RS-IMLE for Multisensory Imitation Learning

Anonymous Submission • ICLR 2026 (Under Review)

Abstract

We present PRISM, a single-pass real-time imitation learning policy that combines batch-global Rejection Sampling IMLE with temporal attention over multisensory histories (RGB, depth, tactile, proprioception, audio). PRISM matches or surpasses state-of-the-art diffusion and flow-based policies on CALVIN, MetaWorld, Robomimic, and real-world loco-manipulation tasks, while being 50× faster at inference and robust to partial modality dropouts. PRISM runs at 30Hz with smooth, multimodal trajectory generation on a Unitree Go2 + D1 arm robot.
